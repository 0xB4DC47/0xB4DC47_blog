WHOAMI

I'm a Senior Security Consultant specializing in hardware and firmware reverse engineering and penetration testing. My journey began with designing and programming low-level systems as a embedded systems engineer. Eventually, after realizing how much I enjoyed reverse engineering and finding security flaws I officially made the switch to pentesting. Nowadays, I lead all sorts pentests from black-box hardware engagements to standard web pentests. I wanted a place to share the things I've learned or fun projects I've worked on, so I decided to make this website.

When I’m not working, I occasionally find myself working on personal projects (probably for this site) including: tinkering with electronics, reverse-engineering, or competing in a CTF. Outside of that, I’ll probably be playing the drums, gaming, or learning a language.

The views expressed here are solely my own and do not reflect those of my employer.
